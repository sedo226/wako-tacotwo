Our History

WakoTaco was founded by Timmy Taco, a local taco connoisseur, in 2016. His vision was to create a taco place that gave customers one of a kind taco recipes that are guaranteed to tickle and delight the taste buds.

Location

WakoTaco is located in the heart of San Francisco on Golden Gate Ave.
